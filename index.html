About Me

My name is Derick Kim and as an UI Designer my passion has always been about creating beautiful designs and going beyond my limitations. My passion for gaming and graphic design made me realize how important the User Experience is to anyone. Whether it be complicated UIs or non-responsive sites, my dream was to be able to incorporate research and my design thinking process to every design I create!
